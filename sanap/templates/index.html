Welcome to the Self-assessment of the adaptation policy process in EEA member countries.

Country coordinators are able to fill in the Self-assessment and submit it online to the European Environment Agency (EEA) using this online platform.

Moreover, this platform is designed in such a way that country coordinators can easily use it to gather responses from their contacts (e.g. researchers, EPAs, NGOs) within their country.

The invitation letter sent out by the EEA to its member countries can be downloaded as a PDF file here. The deadline for filling-in this Self-assessment is YY June 2013.

Sending on the Self-assessment

This platform is designed in such a way that country coordinators can easily use it to gather responses from their contacts (e.g. researchers, EPAs, NGOs) within their country. Please use "Your Contacts" tab to send on the Self-assessment and gather responses.
You will see the responses from your contacts grouped by question in the "Your Contacts" tab.

Please copy and send (e.g. via Email) the generated URL to your contacts. Your contacts can only access and fill-in the survey using that URL and a password is not needed in this case.

Submitting the final version of the Self-assessment to the European Environment Agency (EEA)

Use the "Self-Assessment" tab to fill-in the Self-assessment for the EEA. You can save drafts of the Self-assessment and edit it at any time, but once you Submit the final version of the Self-assessment to the EEA, the Self-assessment will be definitively closed for editing.
Each time you save a draft of the Self-assessment, you can also download a PDF file of it, so that you can print it and send it to colleagues if necessary. When you submit the final version of the Self-assessment to the EEA, you can also download and save a PDF file of it.
