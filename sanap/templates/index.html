Welcome to the Self-assessment of the adaptation policy process in EEA member countries

This platform is designed to easily collect the information about your country. Designated country coordinators are able to fill in the self-assessment survey and instantly deliver it online to the EEA using this online tool.

Moreover, this not only helps the EEA to easier get the responses from the EU countries, but it also helps the country coordinators gather responses from the contacts (experts) inside one's country.

Spreading the survey

Use "Your Contacts"-tab to send the survey and see the answers filled in by your contacts. There you will see a special generated URL you can spread (e.g. via Email). Your contacts/country experts can only access and fill in the survey using that URL.
In the same page you will be able to see those answers, grouped by question.

Submitting the self-assessment to the EEA

Use "Self Assessment"-tab to see your answer to the EEA. You can anytime save drafts (edit) this answer, but once you Submit the final answer to the EEA, the questionnaire will be closed for editing.
Each time you save a draft, you can also download a PDF containing the assessment questions and your answers, so that you can print it and discuss it in meetings.
